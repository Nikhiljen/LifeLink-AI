# LifeLink AI

## AI-Powered Healthcare & Emergency Response Platform

### Project Overview

LifeLink AI is an intelligent healthcare platform designed to connect patients, doctors, hospitals, and emergency services through Artificial Intelligence.

The platform aims to reduce the time required for patients to receive appropriate medical care by helping them find the most suitable healthcare provider based on factors such as doctor specialization, hospital facilities, availability, ratings, patient reviews, and estimated travel time.

LifeLink AI supports multilingual communication through AI-powered translation and voice assistance, enabling users to interact with the system in their preferred local language.

For non-emergency situations, users can:

* Register and manage their medical profile.
* Search nearby doctors and hospitals.
* View doctor ratings and patient reviews.
* Book appointments.
* Chat with doctors.
* Receive AI-powered healthcare guidance.

For emergency situations, the application includes an intelligent SOS system. When activated, the system can:

* Capture the patient's live location (with permission).
* Retrieve the patient's emergency medical profile.
* Recommend the most suitable nearby hospital based on the patient's needs and available resources.
* Notify participating hospitals and ambulance services.
* Notify the patient's emergency contacts.

The current version of LifeLink AI is designed as a Proof of Concept (PoC) using simulated healthcare data to demonstrate the complete workflow.

In future versions, the platform is intended to support integration with:

* Hospital Management Systems
* Ambulance Dispatch Systems
* Government Emergency Services (where official integrations are available)
* Traffic Management Systems
* Police Control Rooms
* Electronic Health Record (EHR) Systems

These future integrations would depend on approvals, partnerships, and the availability of official APIs.
